If you would like to connect more, please follow me on social media or send me an email. I would love to hear from you. Fellowship is essential for our spiritual growth and well-being. In his first letter to the Thessalonians (1 Thessalonians 5:11), Paul tells the church to "encourage one another and build one another up. We are called to be there for one another.
In Proverbs 27:17, King Solomon states, "Iron sharpens iron, and one man sharpens another." We were never meant to go through this life isolated and alone. God created us in His image, and just as he desires a personal relationship with us, we, too, need healthy relationships with other believers.
